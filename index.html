Abel Araya



Most commonly referred to on the internet as Professor AMX, Abel Araya is an entrepreneur, technology consultant, content producer and co-owner of BunaTime who lives in Dallas, Texas. Abel owns and operates LNKS LAB, a local recording studio in the Dallas/Ft. Worth metro area that provides a number of A/V production services and also serves as the official film site for The BunaTime Podcast.

Prior to opening the studio, Abel worked as a software developer for five years and used his in-the-field experience to provide tech support to organizations in the Ethiopian and Eritrean communities, most notably ESFNA, as a tech consultant.

When he isn't glued to a computer screen, Abel spends most of his free time recording and producing his own music, learning new skills and dodging trolls on the internet.
